## Choose Mode page
After the users submit their orders in review page, the app navigates users to the choose mode page.<br>
In the choose mode page, users can either place an order or place a reservation depending on their needs. Specifically, placing a reservation is for those who failed to authenticate the images of their IDs or driver licenses online.
<p align="left">
  <img src="/Images/ChooseMode.png" alt="Choose Mode page" width="327" height="627">
</p>

## Driver Info page
The driver info page happens after a user choose their mode. For those who choose "place an reservation", one need to input their phone number, their names and ID.

  <img src="/Images/DriverInfo.png" alt="Driver Info page" width="327" height="627">
</p>

## Choose Driver page
For those who choose "place an order" option, they will need to authenticate their ID and driver license online through OCR functions. By doing so, they will save time when picking up their vehicles.
